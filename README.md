# Grassroots Impact Ledger (GIL)

Grassroots Impact Ledger (GIL) is a shared digital system designed for young and grassroots NGOs to ethically track activities, beneficiaries, collaboration, and outcomes.

**Created and stewarded by Kendi Muthomi.**

## Why Grassroots Impact Ledger?
Grassroots organizations often work with fragmented records, weak collaboration, and limited evidence for advocacy or accountability. GIL provides a single, shared infrastructure that turns community-level work into credible, usable data.

## Core Capabilities
- Activity & project tracking
- Ethical beneficiary management
- NGO collaboration & teamwork
- Service delivery & impact tracking (PHC, GBV, gender)
- Advocacy & budget evidence generation
- Learning & knowledge management
- Secure data protection & access control
- Public participation & feedback tracking
- Reporting & dashboards
